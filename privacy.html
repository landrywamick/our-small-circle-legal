Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Our Small Circle:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile information you provide
  • Prayer Requests: Prayer requests and updates you create and share
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including prayer activity and engagement
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Send you prayer notifications from your circle members
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about the App
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the App

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Within Your Circle: Prayer requests and activity are visible to members of your prayer circle
  • Anonymous Notifications: When you pray for someone, they receive an anonymous notification
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) to host and operate the App
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase/Firestore, which employs industry-standard security measures. We use encryption for data transmission and storage.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information through the App
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Control your notification preferences
  • Leave prayer circles at any time

6. Children's Privacy

Our Small Circle is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Push Notifications

We use push notifications to alert you when someone in your circle prays for you. You can control notification settings through your device or the App.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase/Firestore: Database and authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may delete your account at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via the App or email.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App's Contact Us page.
